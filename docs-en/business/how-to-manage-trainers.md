# How to Manage Trainers

**Screen**: Business
**Role**: Business Owner
**Difficulty**: Medium

## Overview

Trainers can conduct classes at your sports facilities. When a trainer sends a cooperation request, you'll see it on the Business screen. You can accept it, set an hourly rate, and grant permissions.

---

## Accepting a Trainer

### Step 1: Check Pending Requests

On the Business screen, in the **Trainers** section, you'll see a list of associations with statuses:
- **Pending** (yellow): new requests to review
- **Confirmed** (green): accepted trainers

![Trainer list: Screenshot #31](../../assets/images/business/trainer-associations-list.png)

### Step 2: Set the Hourly Rate

Before or after acceptance, tap the trainer to open the rate panel:
- **Net rate**: hourly rate without VAT
- **VAT rate**: choose: 0%, 8%, or 23%
- **Gross rate**: calculated automatically

![Setting trainer rate: Screenshot #32](../../assets/images/business/trainer-pricing.png)

> **Tip**: You can also enter the gross rate: the net rate will be calculated automatically.

### Step 3: Set Permissions

Configure what the trainer can do at your facility:

| Permission | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Can conduct own training sessions** | Trainer independently creates and manages training sessions |
| **Employee** | Trainer is your full-time employee |
| **Max number of participants** | Participant limit per training session |

![Trainer permissions: Screenshot #33](../../assets/images/business/trainer-permissions.png)

### Step 4: Accept or Reject

- **Accept**: the trainer is added to your team
- **Reject**: the trainer will be notified of the rejection

---

## Managing Existing Trainers

For confirmed trainers, you can:
- **Change the rate**: update the hourly price
- **Change permissions**: adjust what the trainer can do
- **Remove association**: end the cooperation with the trainer

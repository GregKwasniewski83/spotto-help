# Business Screen (Management)

**Business management panel in Spotto**: create a business profile, manage facilities, products, trainers, and payments.

## Screen Overview

The Business screen is the management hub for your sports business. Here you'll find all the tools needed to run your business: from creating a business profile, through managing facilities and schedules, to integrating with payment and invoicing systems.

![Business screen overview: Screenshot #17](../../assets/images/business/business-screen-overview.png)

### What You'll Find on the Business Screen

| Section | Description | More |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Business profile | Company data, avatar, contact details | [Details](./features/business-profile.md) |
| Sports facilities | Managing facilities and schedules | [Details](./features/facility-management.md) |
| Products | Passes, entry packages, subscriptions | [Details](./features/products.md) |
| Agents | Inviting and managing staff | [Details](./features/agent-management.md) |
| Trainers | Trainer associations, rates, permissions | [Details](./features/trainer-associations.md) |

| TPay Integration | Payment gateway configuration | [Details](./features/tpay-integration.md) |
| KSeF Integration | E-invoicing configuration | [Details](./features/ksef-integration.md) |
| Monthly reports | Revenue, reservations, statistics | [Details](./features/monthly-reports.md) |

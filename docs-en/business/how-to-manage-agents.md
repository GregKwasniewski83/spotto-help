# How to Manage Agents

**Screen**: Business
**Role**: Business Owner
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

Agents are people who help you manage your business in Spotto. You can invite employees, grant them access to the management panel, and remove them from the team if needed.

---

## Inviting an Agent

### Step 1: Open the "Agents" Section

On the Business screen, find the **Agents** section and tap **Invite Agent**.

### Step 2: Enter the Email Address

Enter the email address of the person you want to invite. The system will send an invitation to the provided address.

![Inviting an agent: Screenshot #30](../../assets/images/business/invite-agent.png)

### Step 3: Wait for Acceptance

The invitation will appear on the **Pending Invitations** list with information:
- Email address of the invited person
- Invitation date
- Who sent the invitation
- Validity status (invitations expire after a specified time)

> **Tip**: If an invitation has expired, you can resend it using the **Resend** button.

---

## Managing Active Agents

On the **Active Agents** list, you can see:
- Agent's full name
- Email address
- Assignment date
- Who assigned the agent

### Removing an Agent

1. Find the agent on the list
2. Tap the **Remove** button
3. Confirm the removal

> **Important**: After removal, the agent immediately loses access to managing your business.

---

## Managing Invitations

On the **Pending Invitations** list, you can:
- **Resend**: if the person didn't receive the invitation
- **Cancel**: if you want to withdraw the invitation

Expired invitations are marked with a special label.

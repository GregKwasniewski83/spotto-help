# How to Create a Business Profile

**Screen**: Business
**Role**: Business Owner
**Difficulty**: Medium

## Overview

The business profile is your business card in Spotto. It contains company data, address, opening hours, and contact information. Creating a profile is the first step to accepting reservations from customers.

---

## Before You Start

- You must have a Spotto account with an active "Business" role (you can enable it in [profile settings](../profile/how-to-manage-roles.md))
- Prepare your company data: tax ID (NIP), REGON, address, contact details
- If you plan to accept online payments: also prepare data for [TPay configuration](./how-to-setup-tpay.md)

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Business Screen

On the bottom navigation bar, tap the **Business** icon. If you don't have a business profile yet, you'll see the profile creation form.

![Business profile creation screen: Screenshot #18](../../assets/images/business/create-profile-form.png)

### Step 2: Fill in Basic Data

Complete the required fields:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Tax ID (NIP)** | Company tax identification number | Yes |
| **Company name** | Official name from the registry | Yes |
| **Display name** | Name visible to customers | Yes |
| **Address** | Street and number | Yes |
| **City** | City of headquarters | Yes |
| **Postal code** | Postal code (XX-XXX) | Yes |
| **Email** | Contact email address | Yes |
| **Phone** | Contact phone number | Yes |

![Basic data form: Screenshot #19](../../assets/images/business/profile-basic-info.png)

### Step 3: Add a Company Avatar

Tap the camera icon on the profile picture to add a logo or company photo:
- **Take a photo**: use the camera
- **Choose from gallery**: select an existing photo

### Step 4: Set the Schedule

Configure opening hours:
- **Weekdays**: availability hours from Monday to Friday
- **Saturday**: Saturday hours
- **Sunday**: Sunday hours

Each hour (from 00:00 to 23:00) can be marked as available or unavailable.

![Schedule configuration: Screenshot #20](../../assets/images/business/schedule-config.png)

> **Tip**: You can also set special hours for specific dates (e.g., holidays). [How to configure a schedule](./how-to-manage-schedule.md)

### Step 5: Set Location Coordinates

Tap the coordinates field to open the map and indicate the exact location of your business. This will help customers find your facility on the map.

### Step 6: (Optional) Upload a Facility Plan

You can upload a facility plan in PDF format or as a photo. The plan will be visible to customers.

### Step 7: Save the Profile

Tap the **Save** button at the bottom of the form. Your business profile will be created.

---

## Verification

The profile was created correctly when:
- You see the Business screen with the company name and avatar in the header
- Company data is visible in the profile section

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: I can't save the profile
**Solution**: Check that all required fields are filled in. The tax ID (NIP) must have exactly 10 digits.

### Problem: I don't see the Business tab
**Solution**: Make sure you have the "Business" role active. Go to [Profile -> Profile Types](../profile/how-to-manage-roles.md) and enable the business role.

# How to Manage Products

**Screen**: Business
**Role**: Business Owner
**Difficulty**: Medium

## Overview

Products are passes, entry packages, and subscriptions that you offer customers in the Spotto Shop. You can create products with different prices, number of entries, and validity periods.

---

## Creating a Product

### Step 1: Open the "Products" Section

On the Business screen, find the **Products** section and tap **Add Product**.

### Step 2: Fill in Product Data

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Title** | Product name (e.g., "Monthly Pass") | Yes |
| **Subtitle** | Additional description | No |
| **Description** | Detailed product description | Yes |
| **Net price** | Price without VAT (in PLN) | Yes |
| **VAT rate** | 0%, 5%, 8%, 23%, or EX | Yes |
| **Gross price** | Calculated automatically | : |
| **Number of uses** | How many times the customer can use it | Yes |
| **Period** | Days, Weeks, Months, Years, or Lifetime | Yes |
| **Number of periods** | E.g., "3" months | Yes |

![Product form: Screenshot #34](../../assets/images/business/product-form.png)

### Step 3: Set Payment Options

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Paid in app** | Customer can use the product to pay for a reservation |
| **Paid at trainer** | Customer can use the product to pay for a training session |

### Step 4: Assign to Facilities

Choose which facilities the product is valid for:
- **All facilities**: pass valid at all your locations
- **Selected facilities**: select specific facilities from the list

### Step 5: (Optional) Set Dates

You can limit product availability over time:
- **Start date**: from when the product is available in the Shop
- **End date**: until when the product is available in the Shop

### Step 6: Save the Product

Tap **Save**. The product will appear on your list and in the Shop for customers.

---

## Editing and Deleting Products

- **Edit**: tap the product on the list to open the edit form
- **Delete**: tap the delete icon and confirm

> **Important**: Deleting a product does not affect already purchased copies: customers can still use them until they expire.

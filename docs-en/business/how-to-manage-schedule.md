# How to Configure a Schedule

**Screen**: Business
**Role**: Business Owner
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

The schedule defines when your facilities are available for booking. You can set a general weekly schedule and override it for specific dates (e.g., holidays, special events).

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Schedule

On the Business screen, go to the business profile or facility edit view, then find the **Schedule** section.

### Step 2: Set the Weekly Schedule

Configure opening hours for three day types:

| Day Type | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Weekdays** | Monday - Friday |
| **Saturday** | Saturday |
| **Sunday** | Sunday |

For each day type, select the hours (slots from 00:00 to 23:00) when the facility is available.

![Weekly schedule: Screenshot #24](../../assets/images/business/weekly-schedule.png)

> **Tip**: Selected hour = facility available. Deselected = closed.

### Step 3: (Optional) Add Availability for a Specific Date

To override the weekly schedule for a specific date:

1. Select the **Schedule for a specific date** mode
2. Choose a date from the calendar
3. Select availability hours for that day

The date-specific schedule takes higher priority than the weekly schedule.

![Date-specific availability: Screenshot #25](../../assets/images/business/date-specific-schedule.png)

### Step 4: Save Changes

Tap **Save** to confirm the new schedule.

---

## Usage Examples

| Situation | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Holiday closure | Add a specific date with no selected hours |
| Extended weekend hours | Set more slots for Saturday |
| One-time event | Add a date with custom hours |

# How to Generate Monthly Reports

**Screen**: Business
**Role**: Business Owner
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

Monthly reports give you a full picture of your business activity: revenue, number of reservations, cancellations, and a detailed breakdown by facility. You can generate summary and detailed reports.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open Reports

On the Business screen, tap the **Monthly Reports** button.

### Step 2: Select the Period

Choose the month and year for which you want to generate the report.

![Report period selection: Screenshot #35](../../assets/images/business/report-period-select.png)

### Step 3: Choose the Report Type

| Report Type | Content |
|-------------|---------|
| **Summary** | Total revenue, reservations, cancellations, breakdown by facility |
| **Detailed** | Everything from summary + list of reservations, product purchases, trainer sessions |

### Step 4: Review the Report

The report contains:

**Summary:**
- Total revenue (PLN)
- Total number of reservations
- Number of cancellations
- Revenue breakdown by facility (facility name, revenue, reservations, cancellations)

**Detailed report (additionally):**
- List of individual reservations with dates and participants
- Product purchase transactions
- Trainer sessions

![Monthly report: Screenshot #36](../../assets/images/business/monthly-report.png)

### Step 5: Export the Report

You can:
- **Download PDF**: save the report to your device
- **Share**: send the report via email or through another app

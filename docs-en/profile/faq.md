# Frequently Asked Questions: Profile Screen and Settings

## Account

### How can I change my data?

Open the Profile screen and tap **Edit**. You can change your first name, last name, phone number, date of birth, and interests. [Guide](./how-to-edit-profile.md)

### How do I change my password?

Currently, password changes are done through the "Forgot Password" feature on the login screen. More: [Login issues](../troubleshooting/authentication/login-failed.md)

### Can I delete my account?

The account deletion feature is under development. If needed, contact support@spotto.pl.

---

## Avatar

### How do I change my profile picture?

Tap your avatar on the Profile screen. A window with available avatars will appear for you to choose from. [Guide](./how-to-change-avatar.md)

### Can I upload my own photo?

Currently, only predefined animal avatars are available. You can set custom photos in [Business](../business/how-to-create-profile.md) and [Trainer](../trainer/how-to-create-trainer-profile.md) profiles.

## Roles

### What are roles in Spotto?

Roles (profile types) determine which features you have access to:
- **User**: reservations, Shop (always active)
- **Business**: company management
- **Trainer**: conducting classes

[Details](./how-to-manage-roles.md)

### How do I enable the Business or Trainer role?

Open the "Profile types" section on the Profile screen and toggle the switch next to your desired role. When enabling a role for the first time, you must accept the terms and conditions.

### Can I have multiple roles at the same time?

Yes: you can be a User, business owner, and trainer simultaneously.

## Privacy

### What data does Spotto collect?

Spotto collects personal data (name, email, phone), reservation history, and optionally location data. Details can be found in the [Privacy Policy](./features/privacy-settings.md).

### How do I disable location tracking?

Go to Settings > Privacy > Privacy settings and turn off the "Location tracking" toggle. [Guide](./how-to-manage-privacy.md)

### How do I reset analytics data?

In privacy settings, tap **Reset analytics** and confirm. This operation is irreversible.

### How do I unsubscribe from marketing emails?

Go to Settings > Privacy > Privacy settings and turn off "Marketing emails".

---

## Settings

### How do I change the language?

Go to Settings > Preferences > Language. Choose Polish or English. [Guide](./how-to-change-language.md)

### How do I log out of the app?

In Settings, scroll to the bottom and tap **Log out**. Confirm the logout.

### How do I contact support?

In Settings, open the "Help and support" section. Contact details:
- **Support**: support@spotto.pl
- **Complaints**: reklamacje@spotto.pl

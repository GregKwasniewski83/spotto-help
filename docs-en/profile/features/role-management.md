# Role Management

## Description

The role system in Spotto allows you to activate different app features depending on who you are: a user, a business owner, or a trainer. Each role unlocks a separate tab and set of features. You can have all three roles active simultaneously.

## Available Roles

### User

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Status** | Always active: cannot be disabled |
| **Terms** | Must be accepted during registration |
| **Tabs** | Home, Reservations, Shop, Profile |

**User role features:**
- Searching and browsing sports facilities
- Booking facilities (courts, halls, shooting ranges)
- Managing favorite facilities
- Browsing and purchasing products in the Shop (passes, packages)
- Participating in trainings conducted by trainers
- Viewing reservation and purchase history

### Business (Business Owner)

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Status** | Optional: can be enabled/disabled |
| **Terms** | Required when activating the role |
| **Tab** | [Business](../../business/README.md) |

**Business role features:**
- Creating and managing a company profile
- Adding and configuring sports facilities
- Managing schedules and pricing
- Configuring online payments (TPay)
- Managing agents and trainers
- Creating products (passes, packages)
- Generating monthly reports
- KSeF integration (e-invoices)

### Trainer

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Status** | Optional: can be enabled/disabled |
| **Terms** | Required when activating the role |
| **Tab** | [Trainer](../../trainer/README.md) |

**Trainer role features:**
- Creating a trainer profile with description and specializations
- Establishing collaborations with sports businesses
- Creating and managing trainings
- Managing class participants
- Configuring availability schedule
- Setting hourly rates

## Switching Roles

To enable or disable a role:

1. Go to **Profile** > **Settings**
2. Find the **Roles** section
3. Toggle the switch next to the desired role
4. When enabling for the first time: accept the terms and conditions

> 💡 **Tip**: Disabling a role does not cause data loss. If you disable the Business role, your company and facilities remain in the system: after re-enabling, everything will come back.

## Terms and Conditions

Each role has separate terms and conditions that you must accept:

| Terms | When required | Can be declined? |
|-------|---------------|------------------|
| **User Terms** | During account registration | No: required to use the app |
| **Business Terms** | When enabling the Business role | Yes: the role will not be activated |
| **Trainer Terms** | When enabling the Trainer role | Yes: the role will not be activated |

Terms can be expanded and read directly in the app before acceptance. The content of terms may change: the app will notify you when a new version requires re-acceptance.

## What Happens When a Role Is Disabled

| Role | Effect of disabling |
|------|---------------------|
| **Business** | The Business tab disappears from navigation. The company, facilities, and products remain in the system, but you cannot manage them until re-enabling |
| **Trainer** | The Trainer tab disappears from navigation. The trainer profile and business associations remain, but you cannot create new trainings |

> ⚠️ **Important**: The User role is an integral part of the account and cannot be disabled.

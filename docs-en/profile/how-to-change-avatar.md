# How to Change Your Avatar

**Screen**: Profile
**Role**: All
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

Your avatar is your profile picture visible in the app. Spotto offers a set of animal avatars to choose from.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Profile Screen

On the bottom navigation bar, tap the **Profile** icon.

### Step 2: Tap the Avatar

Tap your current avatar in the screen header. The avatar selection window will open.

### Step 3: Choose a New Avatar

Browse the available animal avatars. Tap the one you want to select it.

![Avatar selection: Screenshot #52](../../assets/images/profile/avatar-selection.png)

> 💡 **Tip**: The currently selected avatar is highlighted.

### Step 4: Confirm Your Choice

Tap **Save** or close the window. The avatar will change immediately in the profile header.

# How to Manage Profile Roles

**Screen**: Profile
**Role**: All
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

In Spotto, you have three profile types (roles) that you can enable and disable. Each role gives you access to different app features.

---

## Available Roles

| Role | Description | Can be disabled? |
|------|-------------|------------------|
| **User** | Booking facilities, searching for classes, favorites | No: always active |
| **Business** | Company, facility, and product management | Yes |
| **Trainer** | Conducting trainings, collaborating with businesses | Yes |

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open "Profile Types"

On the Profile screen, find the **Profile types** section.

![Profile types: Screenshot #53](../../assets/images/profile/profile-types.png)

### Step 2: Enable or Disable a Role

Each role has a toggle:
- **User**: always enabled, cannot be disabled
- **Business**: tap the toggle to enable/disable
- **Trainer**: tap the toggle to enable/disable

### Step 3: Accept the Terms and Conditions

When enabling a role for the first time, you must accept the corresponding terms and conditions:
- Expand the terms to read them
- Check the agreement box

> ⚠️ **Important**: The User terms and conditions must always be accepted.

### Step 4: Save Changes

Tap **Save changes**. The roles will be updated.

---

## What Does Each Role Provide?

### User
- Searching for and booking sports facilities
- Managing favorites
- Browsing and purchasing products in the Shop
- Participating in trainings

### Business
- Access to the [Business](../business/README.md) screen
- Creating a company profile and facilities
- Managing products, agents, and trainers
- Monthly reports

### Trainer
- Access to the [Trainer](../trainer/README.md) screen
- Creating a trainer profile
- Conducting classes
- Collaborating with sports facilities

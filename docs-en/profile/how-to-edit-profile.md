# How to Edit Profile Data

**Screen**: Profile
**Role**: All
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

On the Profile screen, you can change your personal data: first name, last name, phone number, date of birth, and sports interests.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Profile Screen

On the bottom navigation bar, tap the **Profile** icon.

### Step 2: Tap "Edit profile"

On the personal data card, tap the **Edit** button. The edit form will open.

![Profile editing: Screenshot #50](../../assets/images/profile/edit-profile-form.png)

### Step 3: Change Your Data

You can change:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **First name** | Your first name |
| **Last name** | Your last name |
| **Phone** | Contact number |
| **Date of birth** | Your date of birth |
| **Interests** | Choose your favorite sports (multiple selection) |

![Sports interests: Screenshot #51](../../assets/images/profile/interests-selection.png)

> 💡 **Tip**: Interests help the app tailor recommendations to your preferences.

### Step 4: Save Changes

Tap **Save**. Your data will be updated immediately.

# Cancellation and Refund Policy

## Description

Spotto allows reservation cancellation with a refund, provided certain rules are met. Below you will find complete information about the cancellation policy.

## Cancellation Rules

### 48-Hour Rule

A reservation can be canceled **no later than 48 hours** before the scheduled visit date.

| Time until reservation | Can it be canceled? |
|------------------------|---------------------|
| > 48 hours | Yes |
| 24-48 hours | No |
| < 24 hours | No |
| Today | No |
| Past | No |

### Who Can Cancel

- Only the person who made the reservation can cancel it
- Reservations paid with a **pass or voucher** cannot be canceled
- Cancellation applies to reservations with status: Confirmed, Pending, Active, Partial

## Cancellation Fee

A fee is charged for each cancellation (full or partial):

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Fee** | 5% of the canceled portion's value |
| **Refund** | 95% of the canceled portion's value |

**Example**: Canceling a 200 PLN reservation
- Fee: 10 PLN (5%)
- Refund: 190 PLN (95%)

## Refund Timeline

- The refund is processed automatically through the TPay system
- Funds return to your account within **3-5 business days**
- The refund goes to the same payment method used for the reservation

## Partial Cancellation

For partial cancellation:
- The 5% fee is charged **only on the canceled slots**
- Remaining slots stay active
- Reservation status changes to "Partial"

**Example**: Reservation of 4 slots at 50 PLN each (200 PLN total), canceling 2 slots:
- Canceled value: 100 PLN
- Fee: 5 PLN (5% of 100 PLN)
- Refund: 95 PLN
- Remaining active value: 100 PLN

## Policy Exceptions

| Situation | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| Pass/voucher | Cancellation not available |
| Facility canceled the reservation | Full 100% refund |
| Technical failure | Contact support |

# How to cancel a reservation

**Screen**: Reservations
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

If your plans change, you can cancel an entire reservation and receive a refund. Cancellation is available for reservations with a future date, subject to the 48-hour rule.

---

## Before You Start

- The reservation must have a **future date** (not today and not in the past)
- There must be **more than 48 hours** until the reservation starts
- The reservation must not be paid with a voucher/pass
- Reservation status must be: confirmed, pending, active, or partially canceled

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open reservation details

On the Reservations screen, tap the card of the reservation you want to cancel.

![IMG_RES_006 : Tap the reservation card](../../assets/images/reservations/tap-reservation-card.png)

### Step 2: Tap "Cancel All"

In the details view, scroll down to the actions section. Tap the **Cancel All** button.

![IMG_RES_007 : Cancel All button](../../assets/images/reservations/cancel-all-button.png)

> ⚠️ **Important**: If the button is inactive (gray), it means the reservation does not qualify for cancellation: check the conditions in the "Before You Start" section.

### Step 3: Confirm cancellation

A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you are sure you want to cancel. Tap **Confirm Cancellation**.

![IMG_RES_008 : Cancellation confirmation dialog](../../assets/images/reservations/cancel-confirmation.png)

### Step 4: Wait for processing

The app will process the cancellation: you will see a loading indicator. Once complete:

- A **cancellation confirmation** will appear with refund details
- Refund amount = 95% of the price (5% cancellation fee)
- The refund will reach your account within 3-5 business days

![IMG_RES_009 : Cancellation confirmation with refund](../../assets/images/reservations/cancel-success.png)

---

## Cancellation Rules

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Deadline** | Min. 48 hours before start |
| **Fee** | 5% of reservation value |
| **Refund** | 95% to your account in 3-5 business days |
| **Passes** | Reservations paid with pass/voucher cannot be canceled |
| **Today/past** | Reservations for today and past ones cannot be canceled |

> 💡 **Tip**: If you want to cancel only some hours, use the [partial cancellation](./how-to-partial-cancel.md) feature.

---

## Verification

Cancellation was successful if:
- You see a confirmation with the refund amount
- Reservation status changed to "Canceled"
- The reservation disappeared from the upcoming list

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Cancel button is inactive
**Solution**: Check whether:
- More than 48 hours remain until the reservation
- The reservation was not paid with a pass
- The reservation is not already canceled

### Problem: Error "Cannot cancel within 48 hours"
**Solution**: The 48h rule is mandatory. If less than 48 hours remain until your reservation, cancellation is not possible. Contact the facility directly.

### Problem: Refund has not arrived after 5 business days
**Solution**: Refunds are processed through the TPay system. If funds have not arrived after 5 days:
1. Check your bank transaction history
2. Contact Spotto support, providing the reservation number

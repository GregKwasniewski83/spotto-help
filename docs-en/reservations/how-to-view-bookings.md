# How to view reservations

**Screen**: Reservations
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

The Reservations screen shows all your upcoming sports facility reservations and trainings, sorted from the nearest. You can quickly check the details of each visit.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Reservations screen

Tap the **calendar** icon on the bottom navigation bar of the app.

![IMG_RES_002 : Navigation bar with Reservations button highlighted](../../assets/images/reservations/nav-bar-reservations.png)

### Step 2: Browse upcoming events

On the screen you will see a list of upcoming events. Each card contains:

- **Photo** : facility avatar or trainer icon
- **Title** : facility or training name
- **Date and time** : formatted as "Today", "Tomorrow", or full date
- **Location** : facility address
- **Duration** : number of booked hours
- **Price** : total cost
- **Type** : "Reservation" or "Training" badge

![IMG_RES_003 : Upcoming reservations list](../../assets/images/reservations/upcoming-list.png)

> 💡 **Tip**: A newly created reservation will appear with a blue border and a pulsing animation so you can easily find it.

### Step 3: See all reservations

If you have more reservations than visible on the screen, tap the **See all** button at the top of the section.

A full-screen list with all upcoming events will open.

### Step 4: Open reservation details

Tap any card to see full details:

- Facility information (name, address, contact)
- Booked hours (time slots)
- Trainer information (if hired)
- Price breakdown
- Payment status
- Reservation number
- Action buttons (payment, cancellation)

---

## Event Types

On the list you will see two types of events:

| Type | Badge | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Reservation** | Blue | Booked sports facility (court, hall, shooting range) |
| **Training** | Green | Trainer-led training you signed up for |

---

## Verification

Browsing works correctly if:
- You can see a list of your upcoming reservations
- Cards contain dates, names, and prices
- You can tap a card to see details

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: The list is empty
**Solution**: If you have no upcoming reservations, you will see a message encouraging you to make your first reservation. Go to the [Home screen](../home/README.md) to book a facility.

### Problem: New reservation doesn't appear
**Solution**: Wait a few seconds: the app synchronizes new reservations with the service (up to 5 attempts). If it still doesn't show, pull the screen down to refresh.

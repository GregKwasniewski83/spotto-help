# Frequently Asked Questions : Reservations Screen

## Viewing Reservations

### How do I check my upcoming reservations?
Tap the calendar icon on the bottom navigation bar. You will see a list of upcoming reservations and trainings sorted by date. [Full guide](./how-to-view-bookings.md)

### Can I also see trainings I signed up for?
Yes. The Reservations screen displays both facility reservations and trainer-led trainings. Each type has a separate color-coded badge.

### Why doesn't my new reservation appear on the list?
The app synchronizes new reservations automatically (up to 5 attempts). Wait a few seconds: the new reservation will appear with a blue border and a pulsing animation.

## Cancellation

### When can I cancel a reservation?
You can cancel a reservation if there are more than 48 hours until it starts. [Full rules](./features/refund-policy.md)

### How much does cancellation cost?
The cancellation fee is 5% of the canceled portion's value. You will receive a 95% refund to your account.

### Can I cancel just one hour from a multi-hour reservation?
Yes: use the [partial cancellation](./how-to-partial-cancel.md) feature. Select specific slots to cancel, and the rest will remain active.

![IMG_RES_021 : Slot selection view for partial cancellation](../../assets/images/reservations/faq-partial-cancel-slots.png)

### Can I cancel a reservation paid with a pass?
No. Reservations paid with a pass or voucher cannot be canceled through the app. Contact the facility directly.

### What happens if the facility cancels my reservation?
You will receive a full 100% refund (no 5% fee). A notification will appear in the app.

## Payments and Refunds

### How long does a refund take?
The refund reaches your account within **3-5 business days** from the moment of cancellation.

### Which account does the refund go to?
The refund goes to the same payment method used for the reservation (card, bank account).

### What are "Pending Payments"?
These are reservations for which payment was not completed. They appear at the top of the Reservations screen. You can complete the payment or cancel the reservation. [How to complete](./how-to-pay-pending.md)

![IMG_RES_020 : Pending payments section on the Reservations screen](../../assets/images/reservations/faq-pending-payments.png)

### What payment methods are available?
Spotto supports payments through TPay: payment cards (Visa, Mastercard), fast bank transfers, and BLIK.

## Statuses

### What are the reservation statuses?

- **Confirmed** : paid, ready
- **Pending** : awaiting payment or confirmation
- **Active** : in progress now
- **Partial** : some slots canceled
- **Canceled** : fully canceled
- **Completed** : visit completed

[Full list of statuses](./features/booking-status.md)

### What does the "Partial" status mean?
It means that some hours from the reservation have been canceled, but the remaining slots are still active.

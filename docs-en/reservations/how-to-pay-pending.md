# How to complete a pending payment

**Screen**: Reservations
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

If you did not complete the payment for a reservation, it will appear in the **Pending Payments** section on the Reservations screen. You can complete the transaction or cancel it.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Find the pending payments section

At the top of the Reservations screen, you will see a section with an alert icon and the heading **Pending Payments**.

![IMG_RES_014 : Pending payments section](../../assets/images/reservations/pending-payments-section.png)

> 💡 The section is displayed only when you have unfinished transactions. If you don't see it: you have no pending payments.

### Step 2: Check payment details

Each pending payment card contains:
- **Description** : facility or training name
- **Amount** : price to pay
- **Time** : when it was created (e.g., "5 minutes ago")
- **Status** : "Awaiting payment"

### Step 3: Tap "Payment"

Tap the blue **Payment** button on the card.

### Step 4: Complete payment in TPay

A browser will open with the TPay payment system. Choose a payment method:
- **Payment card** (Visa, Mastercard)
- **Fast bank transfer**
- **BLIK**

Complete the transaction following the instructions on the TPay page.

### Step 5: Return to the app

After completing the payment, return to the Spotto app. The app will automatically check the payment status.

- **Success** : you will see a green confirmation, the reservation will be confirmed
- **Timeout** : yellow warning, try again in a moment
- **Error** : red message, try a different payment method

![IMG_RES_016 : Payment confirmation](../../assets/images/reservations/payment-success.png)

---

## Canceling a Pending Payment

If you don't want to continue with the reservation:

1. Tap the gray **Cancel** button on the payment card
2. In the confirmation dialog, tap **Cancel Payment**
3. The reservation will be canceled and the card will disappear from the list

---

## Important Information

- Pending payments have a **limited validity period**
- If you don't pay within the designated time, the reservation will be automatically canceled
- The app checks the payment status automatically (up to 30 attempts every 2 seconds)

---

## Verification

Payment was successful if:
- You see a green confirmation
- The card disappeared from the pending payments section
- The reservation appears on the upcoming list with "Confirmed" status

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Payment won't go through
**Solution**: Check your bank account balance, card validity, and try a different method (e.g., BLIK instead of card).

### Problem: After payment I still see "Pending"
**Solution**: Wait a minute and refresh the screen. If the problem persists, check your bank transaction history: if funds were deducted, contact support.

# How to Search for a Sports Facility

**Screen**: Home
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

The search bar on the Home screen lets you quickly find a sports facility in any city. You can filter results by location, date, and facility type (e.g. squash court, shooting range, gym).

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Search Bar

At the top of the Home screen you will see the search bar. Tap it to expand the search form.

![Search bar](../../assets/images/home/search-bar.png)

### Step 2: Enter a City or Location

Type the name of the city where you are looking for a facility (e.g. "Warsaw", "Krakow", "Gdansk").

![Entering a city](../../assets/images/home/search-city-input.png)

> 💡 **Tip**: The app suggests city names as you type — just select from the list.

### Step 3: Choose a Date

Tap the date field to select the day on which you want to book. Today's date is set by default.

![Date picker](../../assets/images/home/search-date-picker.png)

### Step 4: Choose a Facility Type (Optional)

If you are looking for a specific type of facility, select it from the drop-down list:
- Squash court
- Shooting range
- Gym
- Fitness studio
- Football pitch
- and more...

> 💡 **Tip**: If you do not choose a type, you will see all available facilities in the selected city.

### Step 5: Browse the Results

After filling in the filters you will see a list of facilities that match your criteria. Each result card includes:

- **Facility name** : full name of the venue
- **Address** : location with city
- **Availability** : free slots for the selected day
- **Price** : approximate price per hour
- **Rating** : average user rating

![Search results](../../assets/images/home/search-results.png)

### Step 6: Select a Facility

Tap a facility card to see its full details, or use the **Book** button to go directly to the booking flow.

---

## Verification

The search was successful if:
- You can see a list of facilities matching your criteria
- Result cards show names, addresses, and availability
- You can tap a card to view the details

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: No search results
**Solution**: Check that:
- The city name is spelled correctly
- The selected date is not too far in the future
- Try removing the facility type filter to see more results

### Problem: The search bar does not respond
**Solution**: Make sure you have an internet connection. Try closing and reopening the app.

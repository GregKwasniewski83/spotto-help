# How to Use the Map View

**Screen**: Home
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

The map view on the Home screen lets you see sports facilities near you on an interactive map. It is the quickest way to find the nearest facility and book it.

---

## Before You Start

- Make sure the app has access to your location (GPS)
- Check that location is enabled in your phone's settings

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Map View

On the Home screen, find the **Nearby Facilities** section with the map. Tap the map to expand it to full screen.

![Map section](../../assets/images/home/map-section.png)

### Step 2: Allow Location Access

The first time you use the map, the app will ask for access to your location. Tap **Allow** to see facilities near you.

![Location permission](../../assets/images/home/location-permission.png)

> ⚠️ **Important**: Without location access the map will not be able to show facilities in your area. You can still browse the map manually.

### Step 3: Browse Markers on the Map

On the map you will see coloured markers (pins) indicating sports facilities. Each marker corresponds to one facility.

![Map markers](../../assets/images/home/map-markers.png)

- **Zoom in/out** : use the pinch gesture or the +/- buttons
- **Pan** : drag your finger across the map to explore other areas
- **My location** : tap the crosshair icon to return to your position

### Step 4: Tap a Marker

Tap any marker to see basic information about the facility:
- Facility name
- Address
- Distance from you
- Rating

![Marker details](../../assets/images/home/map-marker-details.png)

### Step 5: Go to Details or Book

From the marker information pop-up you can:
- **View details** : open the full facility page
- **Book** : go directly to time slot selection

---

## Verification

The map view is working correctly if:
- The map is displayed with your location (blue dot)
- Sports facility markers are visible
- Tapping a marker shows information about the facility

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: The map does not load
**Solution**:
- Check your internet connection
- Close and reopen the app
- Make sure the app is up to date

### Problem: I cannot see any markers
**Solution**:
- Zoom in — markers may not be visible when zoomed far out
- Check whether there are any registered facilities in your area
- Try panning the map to a larger city

### Problem: My location is inaccurate
**Solution**:
- Enable GPS (precise location) in your phone's settings
- Go outside to get a better GPS signal
- Grant the app access to "precise location" in the permissions settings

# How to Manage Favourite Facilities

**Screen**: Home
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

The favourites system lets you save the sports facilities you visit most often. This means you do not have to search for the same place every time — it is always at hand in the Home screen.

---

## Steps

### Adding a Facility to Favourites

#### Step 1: Find a Facility

Search for a sports facility using the [search bar](./how-to-search-facilities.md) or [categories](./how-to-browse-categories.md).

#### Step 2: Open the Facility Details

Tap the facility card to open its details page.

#### Step 3: Add to Favourites

On the facility details page, tap the heart icon (♡) in the top-right corner. The icon will change to a filled heart (♥), confirming that the facility has been added to your favourites.

![Adding to favourites](../../assets/images/home/add-to-favorites.png)

> 💡 **Tip**: You can also add a facility to favourites directly from the search results card by tapping the heart icon there.

---

### Browsing Favourites on the Home Screen

On the Home screen you will find the **Favourites** section, which displays your saved facilities as quick-access cards.

![Favourites section](../../assets/images/home/favorites-section.png)

From the favourites section you can:
- **Tap a card** : open the facility details
- **Book** : go directly to the booking flow

---

### Removing a Facility from Favourites

#### Step 1: Find the Facility in Favourites

Go to the favourites section on the Home screen or open the facility details page.

#### Step 2: Remove from Favourites

Tap the filled heart icon (♥). The icon will change back to an empty heart (♡), confirming that the facility has been removed from your favourites.

---

## Verification

The favourites system is working correctly if:
- After adding a facility, the heart icon changes to the filled version
- The facility appears in the favourites section on the Home screen
- After removing a facility from favourites, it disappears from the section

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: I cannot see the favourites section on the Home screen
**Solution**: The favourites section only appears after you have added at least one facility. Add a facility to your favourites and the section will appear.

### Problem: Favourites are not being saved
**Solution**: Make sure you are logged in to your account. Favourites are tied to your user account and require an active session.

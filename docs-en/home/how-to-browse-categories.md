# How to Browse Sport Categories

**Screen**: Home
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

On the Home screen you will find a carousel of popular sport categories. Instead of typing a query into the search bar, you can quickly select a sport you are interested in and see all available facilities.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Find the Categories Section

Scroll down the Home screen until you see the **Popular Activities** section. It is displayed as a horizontal carousel with sport icons.

![Categories carousel](../../assets/images/home/categories-carousel.png)

### Step 2: Browse the Available Categories

Swipe left or right on the carousel to see all available categories. Example categories:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Shooting | Sport and recreational shooting ranges |
| Squash | Squash courts |
| Gym | Weight rooms and fitness studios |
| Tennis | Tennis courts |
| Padel | Padel courts |
| Football | Football pitches (outdoor and indoor) |
| Swimming Pool | Swimming pools and aquatic centres |
| Badminton | Badminton courts |

> 💡 **Tip**: The list of categories may vary depending on the availability of facilities in your area.

### Step 3: Select a Category

Tap the category you want. The app will display all facilities offering that sport.

![Category results](../../assets/images/home/category-results.png)

### Step 4: Browse Facilities in the Category

You will see a list of facilities similar to search results. Each facility card includes:
- Name and address
- Available time slots
- Price
- User rating

Tap a facility to see its details, or book a free slot straight away.

---

## Verification

Category browsing is working correctly if:
- You can see the carousel with sport icons on the Home screen
- Tapping a category displays a list of facilities
- The facilities match the selected sport

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: The category carousel does not appear
**Solution**: Make sure the app is updated to the latest version. Try refreshing the Home screen (pull down to refresh).

### Problem: The selected category shows no facilities
**Solution**: This may mean there are no facilities offering that sport in your area. Try:
- Changing the location in the search bar
- Selecting a different category

# Frequently Asked Questions : Home Screen

## Searching for facilities

### How can I find a sports facility near me?

You have two options:
1. **Search bar** : type the name of a city in the search bar at the top of the Home screen and select a date. [Step-by-step guide](./how-to-search-facilities.md)
2. **Map** : open the map view, which will automatically show facilities near your location. [How to use the map](./how-to-use-map.md)

### Can I search for facilities in another city?

Yes. Type any city in the search field : the app does not restrict results to your current location.

### Why is the search bar not showing any results?

![No search results screen](../../assets/images/home/faq-no-search-results.png)

Most common causes:
- City name spelled incorrectly : check the spelling
- No facilities registered in Spotto in that city
- A too-narrow facility type filter selected : remove the filter and try again
- No internet connection

## Sport categories

### What sport categories are available?

Available categories include: Shooting, Squash, Gym, Tennis, Padel, Football, Swimming pool, Badminton, Climbing, and others. The list is dynamic and depends on the facilities in the system. [Full list of categories](./features/categories.md)

### Can I filter facilities by multiple categories at once?

No : currently only one category can be selected at a time. To see facilities from different disciplines, use the search bar without a facility type filter.

## Favourites

### How do I add a facility to favourites?

Tap the heart icon (♡) on the facility card in the search results or on the facility details page. [Full guide](./how-to-manage-favorites.md)

![Heart icon on facility card](../../assets/images/home/faq-heart-icon.png)

### Do my favourites sync across devices?

Yes. Favourites are linked to your account and sync automatically on all devices where you are logged in.

### How many facilities can I add to favourites?

There is no limit : you can add as many facilities as you like.

## Map

### Why is the map not showing my location?

The app needs access to GPS location. Check:
- Whether location is enabled in your phone's settings
- Whether the Spotto app has location permissions
- [How to enable location](./features/map-view.md)

### Can I use the map without location turned on?

Yes, but the map will not show your position or sort facilities by distance. You can manually pan and browse the map.

## Booking

### What is the fastest way to book a facility?

The fastest way is the **Quick Book (QuickBook)** feature : one tap on the facility card in the search results. [How to book](./how-to-book-facility.md)

### Can I book a facility for several hours?

Yes. When selecting a time slot, tap multiple adjacent time slots.

### What are "Pending payments"?

These are reservations for which the payment process was not completed. They appear as a notification on the Home screen. Tap the notification to complete the payment.

![Pending payments notification on Home screen](../../assets/images/home/faq-pending-payments.png)

### What payment methods are accepted?

Spotto supports payments via TPay:
- Payment cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Fast bank transfers
- BLIK

![TPay payment methods](../../assets/images/home/faq-payment-methods.png)

## Community wall

### What is the community wall?

It is a section on the Home screen where users can browse posts, activity, and announcements from the Spotto community. [More about the wall](./features/community-wall.md)

![Community wall section on Home screen](../../assets/images/home/faq-community-wall.png)

### Can I turn off the community wall?

The wall is part of the Home screen and cannot be disabled. You can skip it by scrolling past it.

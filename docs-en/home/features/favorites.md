# Favorites system

## Description

The favorites system allows you to save sports facilities that you regularly visit. Saved facilities are displayed as cards in the **Favorites** section on the Home screen.

## How to add to favorites

You can add a facility to favorites in two ways:

1. **From search results**: tap the heart icon (♡) on the facility card
2. **From the details page**: tap the heart icon in the upper right corner

Once added, the icon changes to a filled heart (♥).

## Favorites section on the Home screen

The section appears on the Home screen after adding at least one facility. It contains:

- **Facility cards**: mini cards with the name, address, and rating
- **Book button**: quick booking without opening the details
- **Scrolling**: if you have many favorites, scroll the list horizontally

## Managing favorites

- **Removing**: tap the filled heart icon (♥) to remove a facility from favorites
- **Order**: facilities are displayed in the order they were added (newest first)
- **Sync**: favorites are linked to your account and sync across devices

## Requirements

- You must be logged in: favorites do not work without an account
- An internet connection is required for syncing

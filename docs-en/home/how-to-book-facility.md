# How to Book a Sports Facility

**Screen**: Home
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Medium

## Overview

From the Home screen you can book a sports facility in several ways: directly from search results, from the category list, from favorites, or from the map view. This guide walks through the full booking process step by step.

---

## Before You Start

- You must have a Spotto account and be logged in
- You need a valid payment method (card or TPay)

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Find a Facility

Use one of the following methods to find a facility:
- [Search](./how-to-search-facilities.md) : enter a city and date
- [Categories](./how-to-browse-categories.md) : choose a sport type
- [Favorites](./how-to-manage-favorites.md) : pick from your saved facilities
- [Map](./how-to-use-map.md) : find one on the map

### Step 2: Tap the "Book" Button

On the facility card (in search results or in the details view) tap the **Book** button.

![Book button](../../assets/images/home/book-button.png)

> 💡 **Tip**: You can also use the **Quick Book** (QuickBook) feature, which lets you reserve a free slot with a single tap directly from search results.

### Step 3: Choose a Time Slot

You will see a calendar with available slots:
- **Green slots** : available for booking
- **Grey slots** : already reserved
- **Yellow slots** : limited availability

Tap the slot you want to select it.

![Time slot selection](../../assets/images/home/time-slot-selection.png)

### Step 4: Review Booking Details

Before confirming you will see a summary:
- Facility name and address
- Selected day and time
- Duration
- **Total price**

![Booking summary](../../assets/images/home/booking-summary.png)

> ⚠️ **Important**: Check the date, time, and price carefully before confirming. Once confirmed, changing the time slot may require cancelling the booking.

### Step 5: Confirm and Pay

Tap the **Confirm Booking** button. You will be redirected to the payment system (TPay), where you can pay by:
- Debit/credit card
- Instant bank transfer
- BLIK

After a successful payment you will see a booking confirmation.

![Booking confirmation](../../assets/images/home/booking-confirmation.png)

---

## Quick Book (QuickBook)

The QuickBook feature lets you book a facility without going into the details view:

1. In search results, tap the **Quick Book** button on the facility card
2. The app will automatically select the nearest available slot
3. Confirm and pay

> 💡 **Tip**: QuickBook is ideal when you have no preference for a specific time and simply want to play as soon as possible.

---

## Pending Payments

If you interrupt the payment process or the payment fails, the booking will appear in the **Pending Payments** section on the Home screen.

To complete the payment:
1. Tap the pending payment notification on the Home screen
2. Go to the payment system
3. Complete the transaction

> ⚠️ **Important**: Pending payments have a limited validity period. If you do not pay within the allotted time, the booking will be automatically cancelled.

---

## Verification

The booking was successful if:
- You see the booking confirmation screen
- The booking appears in the "Upcoming Reservations" section on the Home screen
- You receive a push notification or email confirmation

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: No available time slots
**Solution**: The facility may be fully booked for the selected day. Try:
- Choosing a different day
- Checking later time slots
- Looking for a similar facility nearby

### Problem: Payment failed
**Solution**:
- Check that you have sufficient funds in your account
- Make sure your payment card is valid
- Try a different payment method (e.g. BLIK instead of a card)
- If the problem persists, contact support

### Problem: Booking does not appear after payment
**Solution**: Wait a few minutes — confirmation may require processing time. If the booking does not appear after 15 minutes:
- Check the "Pending Payments" section
- Refresh the Home screen (pull down to refresh)
- Contact support with your transaction number

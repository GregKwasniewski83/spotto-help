# Business Associations

## Description

The association system connects trainers with sports facilities. A trainer sends a partnership request, and the business accepts or rejects it. Once accepted, the trainer can conduct sessions at the business's facilities according to the permissions granted by the owner.

## How the Process Works

1. **You search for a business**: search by city and facility type
2. **You send a request**: the business receives a notification
3. **You wait for a decision**: the business accepts or rejects
4. **Partnership active**: after acceptance, you can conduct sessions

## Association Statuses

| Status | Color | Description | Your Actions |
|--------|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Pending** | Yellow/orange | Request sent, business hasn't decided yet | Resend email, cancel request |
| **Confirmed** | Green | Partnership active: you can conduct sessions | Remove association |
| **Rejected** | Red | Business rejected the request (rejection reason visible) | Send a new request |

## Association Details

For each association, you can see detailed information:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Business name** | Name of the sports business |
| **City** | Business location |
| **Avatar** | Business logo or photo |
| **Status** | Current association status |
| **Request date** | When you sent the partnership request |
| **Confirmation date** | When the business accepted (if confirmed) |
| **Rejection reason** | Why the business rejected (if rejected) |

## Permissions After Acceptance

After your request is accepted, the business sets your permissions. You cannot change them yourself: they are configured on the business side:

| Permission | Description | What It Means for You |
|------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Can conduct own trainings** | Trainer independently creates sessions | You see the business's facilities when creating a training and can assign your sessions to them |
| **Employee** | Trainer is a full-time employee of the business | You cannot create trainings on your own: sessions must be created on the business side |
| **Max. participants** | Participant limit per training | E.g., the business allows you to conduct sessions for max. 10 people |

> ⚠️ **Important**: "Employee" and "Can conduct own trainings" statuses are mutually exclusive: if you are an employee, you cannot create trainings on your own.

## Hourly Rates

The business sets your hourly rate. The business decides how much it pays you: regardless of the rate you set in your trainer profile:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Net rate** | Price per hour excluding VAT |
| **VAT rate** | Tax rate (0%, 8%, or 23%) |
| **Gross rate** | Price per hour including VAT (calculated automatically) |

## Business Search

The search tool lets you find businesses to partner with:

| Filter | Requirements | Description |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **City** | Minimum 3 characters | Enter a city name to see businesses in the area |
| **Facility type** | Optional | Filter by type (gym, court, shooting range, etc.) |

You can send requests to multiple businesses simultaneously. Each business independently decides on acceptance.

## Managing Associations

| Action | When Available | Description |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| **Resend email** | Status: Pending | Sends another notification to the business |
| **Cancel request** | Status: Pending | Withdraws the partnership request |
| **Remove association** | Status: Confirmed | Ends the partnership with the business |

> ⚠️ **Important**: Removing a confirmed association is **irreversible**. You lose access to the business's facilities. To resume the partnership, you must send a new request.

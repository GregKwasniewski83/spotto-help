# How to Manage Training Participants

**Screen**: Trainer
**Role**: Trainer
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

After creating a training, you can view the list of enrolled participants, contact them, and remove them from the session if needed.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Participant List

On the trainings list (**Trainings** tab), find the training and tap **View participants**.

### Step 2: Review the List

For each participant, you can see:
- **Avatar**: photo or default icon
- **Full name**
- **Email**
- **Phone** (if provided)

![Participant list: Screenshot #47](../../assets/images/trainer/participants-list.png)

### Step 3: Contact Participants

Tap a participant's contact details:
- **Email**: opens the email app
- **Phone**: opens the dialer

### Step 4: Remove a Participant

To remove a participant from the training:
1. Tap the **Remove** button next to the participant
2. Confirm the removal in the dialog box

> ⚠️ **Important**: Removing a participant is irreversible. The participant will be removed from the training without additional notification.

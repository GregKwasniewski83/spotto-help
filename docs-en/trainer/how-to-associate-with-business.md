# How to Establish a Partnership with a Sports Facility

**Screen**: Trainer
**Role**: Trainer
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

To conduct sessions at a sports facility, you need to establish a partnership with it. Search for a business, send an association request, and wait for the owner's approval.

---

## Before You Start

- You must have a [trainer profile created](./how-to-create-trainer-profile.md)

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Business Search

On the Trainer screen, go to the **Associated Businesses** tab and tap **Search businesses**.

![Business search: Screenshot #42](../../assets/images/trainer/business-search.png)

### Step 2: Search for a Business

Enter a city and optionally a facility type:
- **City**: type at least 3 characters to see suggestions
- **Facility type**: select from the list (e.g., Shooting range)

Results will show businesses with sports facilities in the selected city.

### Step 3: Select Businesses

You can send requests to multiple businesses at once:
1. Check the businesses you want to partner with
2. Tap **Send requests**

![Business selection: Screenshot #43](../../assets/images/trainer/select-businesses.png)

> 💡 **Tip**: You can send requests to multiple businesses at once. Each business independently decides on approval.

### Step 4: Wait for Approval

Your requests will appear on the associations list with a **Pending** status (yellow label). The business owner must accept your request.

---

## Association Statuses

| Status | Color | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Yellow | Request sent, waiting for the business's response |
| **Confirmed** | Green | Partnership active, you can conduct sessions |
| **Rejected** | Red | The business rejected the request |

## After Approval

When a business accepts your request, you will see next to your association:
- **"Employee" label**: if the business marked you as an employee
- **"Own trainings" label**: if you can create trainings on your own

## Managing Associations

On the associations list, you can:
- **Resend email**: for pending associations
- **Remove association**: end the partnership

> ⚠️ **Important**: Removing an association is irreversible. To partner again, you must send a new request.

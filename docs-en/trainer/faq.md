# Frequently Asked Questions: Trainer Screen

## Trainer Profile

### What is the difference between an independent trainer and an employee?

An **independent trainer** runs their own business: they must provide a tax ID (NIP), business details, and configure TPay. They can create trainings on their own.

A **trainer-employee** works for a sports facility: they need fewer details, but cannot create trainings on their own. Sessions are created for them by the business.

### Can I change my type from employee to independent?

Yes: edit your trainer profile and change the type. You will need to fill in the additional data required for independent trainers.

### How do I change my hourly rate?

Edit your trainer profile and update the net or gross rate. The system will automatically calculate the other value. Note that a business may set its own rate for you independently of yours.

## Business Partnerships

### How can I start conducting sessions at a facility?

1. [Create a trainer profile](./how-to-create-trainer-profile.md)
2. [Search for a business and send a request](./how-to-associate-with-business.md)
3. Wait for the owner's approval
4. Once approved, you can [create trainings](./how-to-create-training.md)

### A business rejected my request: what can I do?

You can contact the business directly to learn the reason for the rejection. Once you've reached an agreement, you can send a new request.

### Can I partner with multiple businesses?

Yes: there is no limit on the number of associations. You can send requests to multiple businesses simultaneously.

## Trainings

### Why can't I create a training?

Most common reasons:
- You are marked as an "Employee": employees cannot create trainings on their own
- You don't have a confirmed association with any business
- You don't have an active facility reservation

### How many sessions can one training have?

There is no limit: a training can have any number of sessions on different dates.

### Can I edit a training that has enrolled participants?

Yes, but the system will display a warning about the impact of changes on participants. You must confirm the edit.

### How do I remove a participant from a training?

Open the training's participant list and tap **Remove** next to the person. [Guide](./how-to-manage-participants.md)

---

## Schedule

### Do I need to set up a schedule?

It is not required, but recommended. A schedule helps businesses and clients know when you are available.

### Does a schedule for a specific date override the general schedule?

Yes: a schedule for a specific date has higher priority than the weekly schedule.

# App Crashes

## Problem

The Spotto app closes unexpectedly, becomes unresponsive, or displays a blank screen.

## Solutions

### Step 1: Restart the app

Close the Spotto app (force close) and reopen it.

**Android**: Multitask button → swipe Spotto up
**iPhone**: Swipe up from the bottom to the middle of the screen → swipe Spotto up

### Step 2: Update the app

Make sure you have the latest version:
- **Android**: Google Play Store → Spotto → Update
- **iPhone**: App Store → Spotto → Update

### Step 3: Restart your phone

Turn your phone off and on again. This resolves memory issues.

### Step 4: Clear cache (Android)

1. Settings → Apps → Spotto
2. Storage → Clear cache

### Step 5: Reinstall

If the problem persists:
1. Uninstall the app
2. Reinstall from the store
3. Log in with your credentials

> 💡 **Tip**: Your data (bookings, purchases, profile) is linked to your account, not the app : you won't lose it after reinstalling.

---

## Reporting Bugs

If you enabled **Crash Reports** in [privacy settings](../../profile/how-to-manage-privacy.md), the app will automatically send a crash report.

You can also email support@spotto.pl with a description of:
- What you were doing in the app before the crash
- Phone model and OS version
- Spotto app version

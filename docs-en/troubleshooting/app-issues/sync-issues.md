# Sync Issues

## Problem

Data in the app is not updating : e.g., after purchasing a product you don't see it in "My Purchases", a booking doesn't appear, or profile changes are not saved.

## Solutions

### Refresh the screen

Swipe down from the top of the list (pull-to-refresh) to force a data refresh.

### Switch between tabs

Go to a different tab and come back : this forces the data to reload.

### Close and reopen the app

Close the app completely (force close) and reopen it.

### Check your internet connection

An unstable internet connection prevents synchronization. Check:
- Wi-Fi or mobile data connection
- Try opening a website in your browser

### Log out and log back in

As a last resort:
1. Open Settings → Log out
2. Log back in with your credentials
3. Data should reload fresh

---

## Push Notifications

If you're not receiving notifications about purchases and bookings:
1. Check that push notifications are enabled in [privacy settings](../../profile/how-to-manage-privacy.md)
2. Check your phone's system settings : make sure Spotto has permission to send notifications

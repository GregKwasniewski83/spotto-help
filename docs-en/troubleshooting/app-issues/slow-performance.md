# Slow App Performance

## Problem

The Spotto app runs slowly : screens take a long time to load, buttons respond with a delay.

## Solutions

### Check your internet connection

Slow connection = slow app. Check:
- Wi-Fi signal strength
- Mobile data coverage
- Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data

### Close unnecessary apps

Other apps running in the background can use up memory and slow down Spotto. Close unnecessary apps.

### Restart your phone

Restarting your phone frees up memory and can significantly improve performance.

### Update the app

Newer versions may contain performance improvements:
- **Android**: Google Play Store → Spotto → Update
- **iPhone**: App Store → Spotto → Update

### Check phone storage

Lack of free space on your phone can slow down all apps. Free up at least 500 MB of space.

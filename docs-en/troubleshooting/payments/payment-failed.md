# Payment Failed

## Problem

You are trying to pay for a booking or product, but the payment ends with an error.

## Most Common Causes and Solutions

### Insufficient funds

**Symptoms**: TPay rejects the transaction

**Solution**: Check your bank account balance and try again or use a different payment method.

### Payment card error

**Symptoms**: Message about card rejection

**Solution**:
1. Check that the card details are correct
2. Make sure the card has not expired
3. Check if you have reached your transaction limit
4. Contact your bank if the problem persists

### BLIK issue

**Symptoms**: BLIK code is not accepted

**Solution**:
1. Make sure the code is current (BLIK is valid for 2 minutes)
2. Generate a new code in your banking app
3. Check if you have BLIK payments enabled at your bank

### Redirect to TPay not working

**Symptoms**: Browser does not open the TPay page

**Solution**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close other apps in the background
3. Update the Spotto app
4. Try again

### Timeout / time expired

**Symptoms**: Payment session expired

**Solution**:
1. Start the payment from the beginning
2. Make sure you have a stable internet connection
3. Have your payment details ready before starting

---

## After a Failed Payment

- Your bank account **should not be charged** after a failed payment
- If you see a charge despite a failed transaction : it may be a temporary hold that will disappear within 24-48 hours
- The booking or product **will not be purchased** : you can try again

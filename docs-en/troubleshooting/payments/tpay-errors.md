# TPay Errors

## Problem

During payment via TPay, you see an error, the page doesn't load, or the redirect doesn't work.

## Most Common Errors

### TPay page doesn't load

**Solution**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close the browser and try again
3. Clear your browser cache

### "Session expired" on the TPay page

**Solution**:
1. Return to the Spotto app
2. Start the payment from the beginning
3. Try to complete the payment within a few minutes

### Error after successful payment

**Symptoms**: TPay confirms the payment, but the app doesn't update the status

**Solution**:
1. Wait a few seconds : the app verifies the payment in the background
2. Close and reopen the app
3. Check the "My Purchases" or "Bookings" tab
4. If the product/booking doesn't appear after a few minutes : contact support@spotto.pl

### Redirect from TPay doesn't return to the app

**Solution**:
1. Manually open the Spotto app
2. Check if the booking or product was registered
3. If not : wait a few minutes and refresh

## Information for Support

If you contact support@spotto.pl, provide:
- Date and time of the transaction
- Amount
- Payment method
- Screenshot of the error (if available)

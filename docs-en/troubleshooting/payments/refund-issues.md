# Refund Issues

## Problem

You are expecting a refund for a canceled booking or product, but the funds have not returned to your account.

## Refund Policy

### Bookings

- Canceling a booking **more than 48 hours** in advance : full refund minus processing fee (10%)
- Canceling **less than 48 hours** before the booking : refund may not apply (depends on the facility's policy)
- Details can be found in the sports facility's terms and conditions

### Products (passes)

- Refund policies are set individually by each sports facility
- Contact the business directly

## Waiting Time

| Payment method | Refund time |
|---------------|-------------|
| Payment card | 3-10 business days |
| Bank transfer | 1-5 business days |
| BLIK | 1-3 business days |

## What To Do

1. **Check the booking status** : make sure the cancellation was confirmed
2. **Wait** : refunds can take up to 10 business days
3. **Check your bank statement** : the refund may appear under a different date
4. **Contact the facility** : you can find the contact details in the booking details
5. **Report the issue** : email support@spotto.pl with the booking number

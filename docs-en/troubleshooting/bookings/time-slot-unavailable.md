# Time Slot Unavailable

## Problem

You want to book a facility, but the selected time slot is unavailable or grayed out.

## Most Common Causes

### Time slot taken by another user

Someone booked this slot before you.

**Solution**: Choose a different time on the same day or check availability on another day.

### Facility closed at the selected time

The facility owner may have closed the facility for that day (holiday, maintenance break, etc.).

**Solution**: Check a different day. Opening hours may vary on weekends and holidays.

### Minimum booking length

Some facilities require a minimum booking of 2 slots (2 hours).

**Solution**: Select the required minimum number of slots.

---

## Tips

- **Gray slots** = taken or unavailable
- **Green slots** = available for booking
- Check several days to find an available slot
- Peak hours (6:00 PM - 9:00 PM) get booked the fastest

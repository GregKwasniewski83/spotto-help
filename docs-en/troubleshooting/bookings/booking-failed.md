# Booking Failed

## Problem

You are trying to book a sports facility, but the booking ends with an error.

## Most Common Causes and Solutions

### Time slot already taken

**Symptoms**: Message about slot unavailability

**Solution**: The selected slot was booked by another user. Choose a different time or day.

### Payment issue

**Symptoms**: Booking is not confirmed after payment

**Solution**: See [Payment failed](../payments/payment-failed.md)

### No internet connection

**Solution**: Check your connection and try again.

### Facility not accepting bookings

**Symptoms**: No available slots

**Solution**:
1. Check the facility's schedule : it may be closed on the selected date
2. Try a different day
3. Contact the facility directly

---

## Pending Payments

If the payment was interrupted, the booking will go to the **Pending Payments** section on the Home screen. You can complete the payment from there.

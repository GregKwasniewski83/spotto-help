# I Can't Cancel a Booking

## Problem

You want to cancel a booking, but the option is not available or an error is displayed.

## Most Common Causes

### Too late to cancel

**Symptoms**: The "Cancel" button is inactive or a message is displayed

**Explanation**: Many facilities do not allow cancellation less than 48 hours before the booking time.

**Solution**: Contact the sports facility directly : you can find the contact details in the booking details.

### Booking has already started

**Explanation**: You cannot cancel a booking that is already in progress or has already taken place.

### Technical issues

**Solution**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close and reopen the app
3. Try again

---

## Cancellation Policy

| Time until booking | Refund |
|-------------------|--------|
| More than 48 hours | Full refund minus 10% processing fee |
| Less than 48 hours | Depends on the facility's policy |
| After the booking time | No refund |

> ⚠️ **Important**: Specific cancellation policies may vary depending on the sports facility.

# I Forgot My Password

## Problem

You don't remember your Spotto account password.

## Solution

### Step 1: Open the login screen

Launch the Spotto app. On the login screen, tap **Forgot password**.

### Step 2: Enter your email address

Type the email address associated with your account and tap **Send link**.

### Step 3: Check your inbox

A password reset link will be sent to your email address. Check:
- Your inbox
- Spam / Junk folder

### Step 4: Set a new password

Open the link from the email and set a new password.

### Step 5: Log in

Return to the app and log in with your new password.

---

## Troubleshooting

### Email doesn't arrive
- Check your Spam folder
- Make sure you entered the correct email address
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Check if an account with that email exists

### Link expired
- Reset links have a limited validity period
- Generate a new link by repeating the steps from the beginning

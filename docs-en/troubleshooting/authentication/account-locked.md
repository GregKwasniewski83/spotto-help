# Account Locked

## Problem

Your account has been temporarily or permanently locked and you cannot log in.

## Most Common Causes

### Too many failed login attempts

**Solution**:
1. Wait 15-30 minutes
2. Try logging in again
3. If you don't remember your password : [reset it](./forgot-password.md)

### Security lock

If the system detected suspicious activity on your account:
1. Check your email inbox : you should have received a notification
2. If it wasn't you trying to log in : change your password immediately
3. Contact support@spotto.pl to unlock your account

---

## Contact Support

If you cannot unlock your account on your own:
- **Email**: support@spotto.pl
- Provide your account email address and a description of the problem

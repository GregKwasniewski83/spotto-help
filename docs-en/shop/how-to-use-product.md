# How to Use a Purchased Product

**Screen**: Shop → My Purchases
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

After purchasing a product (pass, entry package), you can use it when visiting a sports facility. The app generates a verification code that you show to facility staff.

---

## Before You Start

- You must have a purchased, active product (status: Active, green)
- The product must have remaining entries (counter > 0)

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open "My Purchases"

On the Shop screen, tap the **My Purchases** tab at the top of the screen.

![My Purchases tab: Screenshot #8](../../assets/images/shop/my-purchases-tab.png)

### Step 2: Find the active product

In the purchases list, find the product with **Active** status (green label). You can use the filters:
- **All**: all purchases
- **Active**: only active products
- **Inactive**: expired or used up

![Purchases list with filters: Screenshot #9](../../assets/images/shop/my-purchases-list.png)

### Step 3: Tap "Use Product"

On the active product card, tap the **Use Product** button.

> 💡 **Tip**: The button is only available when the product has "Active" status and remaining entries.

### Step 4: Show the verification code

A window will open with the verification code:

- **Verification code**: large letters and numbers, displayed in a clear font
- **Instructions**: "Show this code to facility staff"
- **Product details**: name, business, price
- **Business contact details**: phone (tap to call), email, address
- **Remaining entries**: usage counter (e.g., "7 of 10")
- **Dates**: purchase date and expiration date

![Product verification code: Screenshot #10](../../assets/images/shop/product-verification-code.png)

> ⚠️ **Important**: Do not close this window until the staff member has verified your code.

### Step 5: Staff confirms usage

After the facility staff verifies the code:
1. The remaining entries counter will decrease by 1
2. You'll see the updated entry count

---

## Verification

The product was successfully used when:
- The "Remaining entries" counter decreased by 1
- You receive confirmation in the app

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Use Product" button is inactive
**Solution**: Check that the product has "Active" status and that you haven't used all entries. A product with "Expired" or "Used Up" status cannot be used.

### Problem: Verification code doesn't work with staff
**Solution**: Make sure you're showing the current code from the app. If the problem persists, contact the business: you can find their contact details in the verification code window.

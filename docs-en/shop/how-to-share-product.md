# How to Share a Product with a Friend

**Screen**: Shop
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

Found a great pass or entry package? You can share a product link with friends. The person who receives the link will be able to open the product directly in the Spotto app.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Find the product in the Shop

Open the **Shop** tab and find the product you want to share.

### Step 2: Tap the share icon

On the product card, tap the **share** icon (upward arrow or three connected dots, depending on your system).

![Share button on product card: Screenshot #11](../../assets/images/shop/share-product-button.png)

### Step 3: Choose how to send

The system share menu will open. You can send the link via:
- SMS / text message
- WhatsApp, Messenger, or another messaging app
- Email
- Copy link to clipboard

### Step 4: Friend opens the link

The person who receives the link:
- If they have the Spotto app installed: the link will open directly in the app on the product page
- If they don't have the app: the link will open a web page with product information

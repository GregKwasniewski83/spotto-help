# How to Buy a Product (Pass/Package)

**Screen**: Shop
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Medium

## Overview

In the Spotto Shop, you can buy passes, entry packages, and subscriptions offered by sports facilities. After purchase, the product goes to "My Purchases" and you can use it when visiting the facility.

---

## Before You Start

- You must have a Spotto account and be logged in
- You need access to a payment method (card, bank transfer, or BLIK via TPay)

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Shop tab

On the bottom navigation bar, tap the **Shop** icon. You'll see a list of available products.

![Shop screen with product list: Screenshot #2](../../assets/images/shop/shop-product-list.png)

### Step 2: Find the product you're interested in

Browse the product list or use the search:
- Tap the **magnifying glass** icon at the top of the screen
- Type the product name, facility name, or city
- Results filter automatically

![Product search: Screenshot #3](../../assets/images/shop/shop-search.png)

> 💡 **Tip**: You can also use filters to narrow results by price, validity period, or business. [How to filter products](./how-to-filter-products.md)

### Step 3: Review product details

On the product card, you'll see:
- **Product name**: title and subtitle
- **Price**: gross amount (including VAT)
- **Number of entries**: how many times you can use it (e.g., "10 uses")
- **Validity period**: how long the product is active (e.g., "3 months")
- **Business name**: which facility offers the product

![Product card: Screenshot #4](../../assets/images/shop/shop-product-card.png)

### Step 4: Tap "Buy Now"

On the product card, tap the **Buy Now** button. A confirmation window will appear with the price and product name.

![Purchase confirmation: Screenshot #5](../../assets/images/shop/shop-purchase-confirm.png)

> ⚠️ **Important**: Verify the price and product name before confirming.

### Step 5: Pay via TPay

After confirmation, you'll be redirected to the TPay payment system. Available methods:
- **Credit/debit card**
- **Instant bank transfer**
- **BLIK**

![TPay payment page: Screenshot #6](../../assets/images/shop/shop-tpay-payment.png)

### Step 6: Confirm the purchase

After successful payment:
1. You'll see a success message
2. The app will automatically switch to the **My Purchases** tab
3. Your new product will appear on the list with **Active** status

![Purchase confirmation: Screenshot #7](../../assets/images/shop/shop-purchase-success.png)

---

## Verification

Your purchase is complete when:
- You see a success message
- The product appears in the "My Purchases" tab with **Active** status (green)
- The usage counter shows the full number of entries

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Payment failed
**Solution**: Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, try a different payment method. [More about payment issues](../troubleshooting/payments/payment-failed.md)

### Problem: Product doesn't appear after purchase
**Solution**: Wait a few seconds: the app automatically refreshes the list. If the product still doesn't appear, close and reopen the app.

### Problem: Redirect to TPay doesn't work
**Solution**: Make sure you have an up-to-date browser installed. Try again or use a different payment method.

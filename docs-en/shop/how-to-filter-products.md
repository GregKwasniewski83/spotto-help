# How to Filter and Search Products

**Screen**: Shop
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

The Spotto Shop offers a search bar and advanced filters to help you quickly find the right product. You can search by name, business, city, and also filter by price and validity period.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the search bar

On the Shop screen, tap the search field at the top of the screen. Type your search phrase: results filter automatically by:
- Product name
- Product description
- Business name
- City

### Step 2: Open the filter panel

Tap the **filter** icon next to the search field. The filter panel will open with filtering options.

![Filter panel: Screenshot #15](../../assets/images/shop/filter-panel.png)

### Step 3: Set filters

Available filters:

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Business** | Show products from a specific facility |
| **City** | Show products from a selected city |
| **Minimum price** | Products more expensive than the specified amount |
| **Maximum price** | Products cheaper than the specified amount |
| **Validity period** | Days, Weeks, Months, Years, or Lifetime |

### Step 4: Choose sorting

You can sort results by:

| Sorting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Newest** | From newest products |
| **Price: ascending** | From cheapest |
| **Price: descending** | From most expensive |
| **Name: A-Z** | Alphabetically |
| **Uses: ascending** | From fewest entries |
| **Uses: descending** | From most entries |

### Step 5: Apply filters

Tap the **Apply** button or close the filter panel. The product list will refresh according to the selected criteria.

> 💡 **Tip**: To reset filters, open the panel again and tap **Clear Filters**.

---

## Filtering by Business

If you navigate to the Shop from a specific sports facility profile, products will be automatically filtered to that facility's offerings. At the top, you'll see a banner **"Products from selected facility"**. Tap **X** to remove the filter.

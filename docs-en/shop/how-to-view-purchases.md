# How to View My Purchases

**Screen**: Shop → My Purchases
**Role**: User
**Difficulty**: Easy

## Overview

The "My Purchases" tab shows all products you've bought in the Shop. You can check the status of each product, how many entries you have left, and when the product expires.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Shop tab

On the bottom navigation bar, tap the **Shop** icon.

### Step 2: Go to "My Purchases"

At the top of the screen, you'll see two tabs. Tap **My Purchases**.

### Step 3: Browse your purchases

Each purchase card contains:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Product name** | Product title and subtitle |
| **Status** | Active (green), Expired (yellow), or Used Up (red) |
| **Progress bar** | Visual representation of remaining entries |
| **Purchase date** | When you bought the product |
| **Expiration date** | Until when the product is active |
| **Business** | Name of the facility offering the product |

![Purchase card with details: Screenshot #13](../../assets/images/shop/purchase-card-details.png)

### Step 4: Filter purchases

Use the filter buttons above the list:
- **All**: show all purchases
- **Active**: only products you can still use
- **Inactive**: expired or fully used products

---

## Product Statuses

| Status | Color | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Active** | Green | Product is valid and has remaining entries |
| **Expired** | Yellow | Product validity period has passed |
| **Used Up** | Red | All entries have been consumed |

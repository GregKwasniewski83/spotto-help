# Frequently Asked Questions: Shop Screen

## Purchasing

### What is a product in the Spotto Shop?

A product is a pass, entry package, or subscription offered by a sports facility. When you buy a product, you receive a specific number of entries that you can use within a given time period.

### What types of products are available?

Products vary by validity period:
- **Daily/weekly**: short-term packages
- **Monthly**: the most popular passes
- **Annual**: long-term subscriptions
- **Lifetime**: unlimited-duration passes

### Can I buy a product for someone else?

No: products are linked to your account. However, you can [share a product link](./how-to-share-product.md) so another person can buy it for themselves.

---

## Payments

### What payment methods are available?

Payments are handled by the TPay system. Available methods include:
- Credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard)
- Instant bank transfer
- BLIK

### Is my payment information secure?

Yes. Payments are processed by the certified TPay gateway. Spotto does not store your card details: all data goes directly to TPay.

### What happens if the payment fails?

The purchase will not be completed, and your account will not be charged. You can try again or choose a different payment method. [Troubleshooting payment issues](../troubleshooting/payments/payment-failed.md)

### Can I get an invoice?

Invoicing depends on the sports facility. Contact the business directly: you can find their contact details in the purchase details under "My Purchases".

## Using Products

### How do I use a purchased product?

Open "My Purchases", find the active product, and tap "Use Product". Show the displayed verification code to the facility staff. [Detailed guide](./how-to-use-product.md)

### How many entries do I have left?

On the purchase card in "My Purchases", you'll see a progress bar and counter (e.g., "7 of 10 entries remaining").

### Can I use a product at different locations of the same business?

It depends on the product:
- Products marked as "All facilities": yes, at all business locations
- Products assigned to specific facilities: only at selected locations

---

## Product Validity

### What happens when a product expires?

The product status changes to "Expired" (yellow label). Unused entries are forfeited. There is no option to extend the product: you need to buy a new one.

### Can I return a product?

Return policies are set by individual sports facilities. Contact the business directly to learn about return options.

### When does the validity period start?

The validity period starts from the purchase date. For example: if you buy a monthly pass on March 15, it will be valid until April 15.

# Troubleshooting: Shop Screen

## Purchase Issues

### Payment failed

**Symptoms**: After being redirected to TPay, you see an error or are redirected back without confirmation.

**Solutions**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Make sure you have sufficient funds in your account
3. Try a different payment method (BLIK instead of card or vice versa)
4. Close and reopen the app, then try again

### TPay page doesn't open

**Symptoms**: After tapping "Buy Now", nothing happens or the browser doesn't open the page.

**Solutions**:
1. Make sure you have an up-to-date browser installed
2. Close other background apps (free up memory)
3. Restart the Spotto app

### Double charge on account

**Symptoms**: You see two charges for the same product in your bank account.

**Solutions**:
1. Check in "My Purchases" whether the product was purchased twice
2. One of the charges may be a temporary hold: it usually disappears within 24-48 hours
3. Contact customer support: support@spotto.pl

## Product Issues

### Product doesn't appear after purchase

**Symptoms**: Payment completed successfully, but the product isn't visible in "My Purchases".

**Solutions**:
1. Wait a few seconds: the app automatically refreshes the list
2. Switch between the "Shop" and "My Purchases" tabs
3. Close and reopen the app
4. Check the filter: make sure it's set to "All" or "Active"

### Verification code doesn't work

**Symptoms**: Facility staff cannot verify the code.

**Solutions**:
1. Make sure you're showing the current code from the app (not a screenshot)
2. Check that the product is still active (green status)
3. Check that you have remaining entries
4. Contact the business: contact details are in the verification code window

### Product expired too early

**Symptoms**: The product status changed to "Expired" even though you expected a longer validity period.

**Solutions**:
1. Check the expiration date on the purchase card: the period is counted from the purchase date
2. If the date is incorrect, contact the business or write to support@spotto.pl

## Search Issues

### I don't see any products

**Symptoms**: The product list is empty.

**Solutions**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Remove active filters (tap "Clear Filters")
3. Clear the search field
4. If you have a business filter enabled (banner at the top), tap X to remove it

### I can't find a product from a specific facility

**Solution**: Use the "Business" filter in the filter panel, or navigate to the Shop directly from the facility profile (on the Home screen).
